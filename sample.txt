RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Residential Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on March 15, 2024, between Sunshine Properties LLC, a limited liability company organized under the laws of California ("Landlord"), and Sarah Michelle Johnson and Robert David Thompson ("Tenants").

PROPERTY DETAILS

The Landlord agrees to lease to the Tenants the residential property located at 1247 Maple Street, Apartment 3B, San Francisco, California 94102 ("Property"). The Property consists of a two-bedroom, one-bathroom apartment with approximately 850 square feet of living space.

LEASE TERM AND RENT

The lease term shall commence on April 1, 2024, and shall terminate on March 31, 2025, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Agreement. The monthly rent is $3,200.00, due and payable on the first day of each month. A late fee of $75.00 will be charged for any payment received after the 5th day of the month.

SECURITY DEPOSIT

Tenants shall pay a security deposit of $3,200.00 upon signing this Agreement. The security deposit shall be held in a separate account at First National Bank and shall be returned within 21 days after termination of the lease, less any deductions for damages, unpaid rent, or other charges as permitted by law.

UTILITIES AND SERVICES

Tenants are responsible for payment of electricity, gas, internet, and cable services. Landlord shall pay for water, sewer, and garbage collection services. The estimated monthly utility costs are approximately $150.00 to $200.00.

TENANT OBLIGATIONS

Tenants shall:
- Maintain the Property in clean and sanitary condition
- Not disturb other tenants or neighbors
- Not sublease or assign this Agreement without written consent from Landlord
- Obtain renter's insurance with minimum coverage of $100,000.00
- Not keep pets without prior written approval and payment of additional deposit of $500.00

LANDLORD OBLIGATIONS  

Landlord shall:
- Maintain the Property in habitable condition
- Make necessary repairs within 72 hours of written notice
- Provide 24-hour advance notice before entering the Property
- Comply with all applicable housing codes and regulations
- Maintain common areas in safe and clean condition

TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice. Tenants may terminate early by paying a fee equal to two months' rent ($6,400.00). In case of breach of this Agreement, the non-breaching party may terminate with 3 days written notice.

ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

Parking: One assigned parking space (#15) is included. Additional parking spaces are available for $125.00 per month.

Storage: A storage unit in the basement (Unit B-12) is included at no additional charge.

Renewal Option: Tenants have the right to renew this lease for an additional one-year term by providing written notice 60 days before expiration. Rent for renewal term shall not exceed $3,360.00 per month (5% increase maximum).

DEFAULT AND REMEDIES

If Tenants fail to pay rent when due, Landlord may charge interest at the rate of 1.5% per month on overdue amounts. Continued non-payment may result in eviction proceedings and additional legal fees of up to $2,500.00.

GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California and the local ordinances of San Francisco. Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration conducted by the American Arbitration Association.
